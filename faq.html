Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our plumbing services.

What areas do you serve?

We serve coastal North Carolina and surrounding areas. Visit our Service Areas page for details.

Do you handle commercial new construction projects?

Yes. We support commercial new construction and facility plumbing needs. Contact us with your project scope, location, and timeline.

Do you offer residential plumbing services?

Yes. We provide repairs, replacements, installations, and maintenance for residential plumbing systems.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We operate under a properly licensed business and carry insurance for covered work.

How do I request a commercial bid or residential quote?

Use our contact form or call us with your scope, location, and timeline. We’ll follow up during business hours.

Do you provide emergency plumbing services?

Please contact us as soon as possible and we’ll do our best to assist based on availability.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We provide free estimates for many projects. Share your details and we’ll advise on next steps.
