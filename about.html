About Us

Honest Plumbing You Can
Rely On

Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical is a husband-and-wife owned company built on quality workmanship, clear communication, and dependable service. After relocating to North Carolina from California, we started Seaside with one goal: provide plumbing done the right way—no shortcuts, no pressure, and no unnecessary upsells.

We show up when we say we will, explain the issue in plain language, and give honest recommendations before any work begins. Whether it’s a small repair or a larger job, we take pride in craftsmanship and treat every home or facility with respect.

When you call Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical, you’re hiring a local team that stands behind its work.
