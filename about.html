About Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical

Honest Plumbing You
Can Rely On

Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical is a husband-and-wife owned plumbing company built on quality workmanship, clear communication, and dependable service. After relocating to North Carolina from California, we started Seaside with one goal: provide plumbing done the right way—no shortcuts, no pressure, and no unnecessary upsells.

We understand that plumbing issues can disrupt daily life at home and operations at work. That's why we focus on reliability, transparency, and doing the job correctly the first time. We show up when we say we will, explain issues in plain language, and provide honest recommendations so you can make informed decisions before any work begins.

Whether we're handling a residential repair, routine maintenance, or a commercial project, we take pride in clean, code-compliant work and treat every home and facility with respect. We work closely with homeowners, property managers, business owners, and facility teams to deliver dependable solutions that last.

When you call Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical, you're hiring a local, family-owned team that stands behind its work and is committed to keeping your home or business running smoothly.
